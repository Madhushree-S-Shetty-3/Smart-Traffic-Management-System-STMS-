About


The Smart Traffic Management (STM) System is designed to address the challenges of urban traffic congestion through innovative and technology-driven solutions. By integrating cutting-edge technologies such as IoT and AI, STM aims to optimize traffic flow, reduce waiting times at signals, and enhance commuter experiences.

Monitor and analyze traffic flow using advanced sensors and cameras to reduce congestion.
